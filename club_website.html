Welcome to the Tech4Care Club

Welcome to the Tech4Care Club, where innovation meets compassion. We are a student-led organization dedicated to exploring how artificial intelligence and behavioral science can work together to improve healthcare, accessibility, and emotional well-being. Our projects range from real-time computer vision systems to ethical behavioral experiments—all powered by devices like the Jetson Orin Nano and open-source AI tools. Through teamwork, curiosity, and responsible design, we aim to create technologies that make a real difference in people’s lives. Explore our site to learn about our projects, research focus, and ways to get involved in building a more caring future through tech. Also, we have a cooperation with Lawrence Rehabilitation, so our devices would truly help the community and make the community a better place!
